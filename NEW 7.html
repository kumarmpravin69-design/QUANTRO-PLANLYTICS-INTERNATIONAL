Our Engineering Services

Core Engineering Modules

1. Structural, Architectural & MEP drawings

We specialize in preparing high-quality Structural, Architectural & MEP drawings for new developments, modification works, and extension projects. Our team ensures that all drawings are precise, compliant with standards, and aligned with project requirements.

2. Authority Approvals & Coordination

We provide complete support in obtaining approved drawings from the concerned authorities. This includes preparing required documentation, coordinating submissions, and ensuring timely approvals to avoid project delays.

3. As-Built Drawings Preparation

We deliver accurate As-Built drawings that reflect the final constructed conditions of the project. These documents are prepared with high attention to detail and verified against actual site execution.
